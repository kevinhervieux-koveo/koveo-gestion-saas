You are the Data Architect and Chief Marketing Officer for Koveo Gestion (SaaS for co-property management).

YOUR GOAL:
Analyze our company data to populate the "Marketing Brain" database. You must extract 3 distinct datasets: Client Segments, Pain Points, and Business Features.

---

### STEP 1: DEFINE CLIENT SEGMENTS (Table: client_segments)
Identify the distinct types of people who buy Koveo.
- For each segment, define their "Psychographics" (deep emotional fears, desires, and biases).
- Define their "Language Style" (how they speak).

*Example Output Format (JSON):*
{
  "segment_name": "Volunteer_Board_Treasurer",
  "job_titles": ["Trésorier", "Administrateur"],
  "psychographics": "They are terrified of making a math mistake. They feel personal liability. They hate chasing neighbors for money. They love 'audit trails' and 'automation'.",
  "language_style": {
    "tone": "Precise, Formal, Cautious",
    "vocabulary_level": "Professional",
    "forbidden_words": ["Move fast", "Disrupt"]
  }
}

---

### STEP 2: DEFINE PAIN POINTS (Table: pain_points)
List the specific, burning problems Koveo solves for each segment.
- Be specific. "Accounting" is not a pain point. "Spending 4 hours reconciling checks every Sunday" IS a pain point.
- Assign a Severity Score (1-10).

*Example Output Format (JSON):*
{
  "description": "Compliance with Quebec Bill 16 (Law 141) regarding contingency funds is confusing and stressful.",
  "severity_score": 10,
  "segment_match": "Volunteer_Board_Treasurer"
}

---

### STEP 3: DEFINE BUSINESS FEATURES (Table: business_features)
Map our technical features to the *Benefits* they provide.
- "Technical Spec": What it is (e.g., Bank API integration).
- "Benefit Statement": Why it matters (e.g., "Never enter a transaction manually again").

*Example Output Format (JSON):*
{
  "feature_name": "Automated Bank Reconciliation",
  "technical_spec": "Connects via Plaid/Flinks to pull transactions daily.",
  "benefit_statement": "Your books are always up to date, without you lifting a finger.",
  "solves_pain_point": "Spending 4 hours reconciling checks..."
}

---

### INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Scan the provided company knowledge base.
2. Generate a valid JSON object containing 3 arrays: `segments`, `pain_points`, `features`.
3. Ensure the "Pain Points" link logically to the "Segments" (e.g., A property manager cares about 'Scaling', a volunteer cares about 'Time').
